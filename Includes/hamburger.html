Hamburger

A classic, juicy hamburger made with seasoned ground beef, grilled or pan-seared to perfection. Served on a toasted bun with fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments. Simple, delicious, and satisfying! üçî

Ingredients üõí

For the Burger:
  • 500 g (1 lb 2 oz) regular minced (ground) beef
  • ¬Ω cup (30 g) panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¬Ω tsp cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 tbsp water (optional)
To Serve:
  • 4 brioche rolls, halved and toasted
  • 2 tomatoes, finely sliced
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 little gem lettuce, leaves picked and washed
  • 4 teaspoons ketchup

Preparation Instructions üë©‚Äçüç≥

  1. In a large bowl, combine the minced beef, panko breadcrumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, ketchup, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Mix well, adding 1-2 tablespoons of water if needed for consistency.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  5. Toast the brioche rolls in a dry pan or under a grill until golden.
  6. Assemble the burgers: Place lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by a patty, tomato slices, red onion, and ketchup.
  7. Top with the other half of the brioche roll and serve immediately.

üí° Tips & Tricks

  • For extra juiciness, let the patties rest for a few minutes before assembling the burger.
  • Customize with your favorite toppings like cheese, pickles, or special sauces.
  • If grilling, oil the grates lightly to prevent sticking.
